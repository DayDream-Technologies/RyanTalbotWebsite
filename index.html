BIO

Ryan Talbot is an American track and field athlete competing in the Decathlon. Born in Alto, Michigan, he attended Forest Hills Central, following in the footsteps of his mother, a former heptathlete. In 2022, he set the MSU decathlon record with 8064 points and earned bronze at the 2023 Pan American Games.

Fact #1 about Track and Field: It dates back to the ancient Olympic Games in Greece.
